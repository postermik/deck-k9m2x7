Problem

The tools don't exist on either side.

Founders start from scratch every raise.
Investors drown in inbound with no triage.
Nobody is building the layer that sits between the two.

Founders Pre-seed / Seed

  • ChatGPT writes paragraphs, not pitches.No sense of what investors actually push on, so the output sounds polished but doesn't survive a first meeting.
  • Templates give layout, not thesis.Pretty slides with zero judgment on whether the argument holds up under scrutiny.
  • Consultants charge $5-15K over 3-4 weeks.Out of reach at pre-seed. Founders still walk into the room unprepared.

Investors Angels / Emerging managers

  • Inbound piles up with no triage.Every deck needs an hour of careful reading before an investor knows if a meeting is worth taking.
  • Manual assessment doesn't scale.Solo angels and emerging managers can't review deal flow the way institutional firms can.
  • No standardized evaluation.Every partner scores decks their own way. Nothing is comparable. Nothing compounds across deals.

Why now

Two shifts, same 18-month window.

The technical unlock and the market unlock are both measurable. Both new.

01
Reasoning models hit the bar for VC-grade judgment.
Before late 2024, no model could produce the auditable reasoning chain that fundraising advice requires. Claude and GPT now score decks the way a partner would, with sources and confidence. The feature Rhetoric depends on didn't exist two years ago.
02
Solo GPs crossed 50% of new VC funds in 2024.
Up from a minority in 2020. 69% of new funds in 2024 were under $25M. A structurally new class of back-office-less capital allocators, with sustained need for tooling they can't build themselves. Nobody is building for them.
